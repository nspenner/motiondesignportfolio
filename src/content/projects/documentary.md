## Project Overview

Urban Stories is a documentary that dives deep into the world of street art and the artists who use the city as their canvas. This project required sensitive storytelling and careful pacing.

## My Role

- Editor
- Colorist
- Sound Designer

## Approach

We spent months following various artists, capturing their creative process and personal stories. The editing process involved weaving together multiple narratives into a cohesive whole.

## Technical Details

- Shot on Canon C70
- Edited in Adobe Premiere Pro
- Color grading in DaVinci Resolve
- Audio post-production in Adobe Audition
