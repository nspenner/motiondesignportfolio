## Project Overview

This music video brings the song "Electric Dreams" to life through a combination of practical effects, motion graphics, and creative cinematography.

## My Role

- Video Editor
- Motion Graphics Artist
- Visual Effects Supervisor

## Creative Approach

The concept centered around the idea of dreams manifesting in the real world, blending reality with surreal visual elements. We used a combination of in-camera effects and post-production magic.

## Technical Details

- Multiple camera setup with Sony A7S III
- Color grading in DaVinci Resolve
- Motion tracking and VFX in After Effects
- Final edit in Premiere Pro
